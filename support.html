FAQ

Is there a free version?

Yes — unlimited habits and check-ins are free forever. Paying unlocks folders, advanced stats, export, and (soon) themes/Watch.

If I buy the lifetime option, will you ever charge me again?

No. Your purchase price is locked in for that product, forever. See the Price Promise page inside the app.

Can I get my data out?

Yes, anytime — Settings > Export Data, CSV or JSON.

How do I restore my purchase on a new device?

Settings > Restore Purchases.
