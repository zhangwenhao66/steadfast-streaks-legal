Privacy Policy — Keepdaily

Last updated: July 8, 2026

Keepdaily does not require an account and does not collect personal information to use the app.

Data stored on your device

Your habits, categories, and check-in history are stored locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework. This data is never uploaded to our servers — we don't have servers that store your habit data. You can export a full copy of your data (CSV/JSON) at any time from within the app, and delete the app to remove all local data.

Purchases

Purchases (one-time or subscription) are processed by Apple and RevenueCat. RevenueCat receives anonymized purchase and subscription status information necessary to verify your entitlement to premium features. See RevenueCat's privacy policy at https://www.revenuecat.com/privacy/ for details.

Analytics

We use TelemetryDeck, a privacy-focused analytics provider, to understand anonymous, aggregate app usage (e.g., how many people open the app, whether a purchase was completed). TelemetryDeck does not use device identifiers or cookies to track you across apps or websites. See https://telemetrydeck.com/privacy/ for details.

No advertising

Keepdaily does not show ads and does not share your data with advertisers.
